On September 22, 2165, Leo Samson, the elusive leader of Heaven’s Fall, was confirmed dead following the failed siege of Cloud Tower, an operation carried out by forces of the Alfornan Military. Official reports remain incomplete. No clear account of his final moments has been released. What has been recovered are the bodies; dozens of Heaven’s Fall operatives, scattered across the lower levels of the tower, many found in positions suggesting they never attempted to flee. Authorities claim the group has been effectively dismantled. Remaining members, if any, are believed to be disorganized and in hiding. Yet questions linger. Eyewitnesses report unusual silence at the scene in the hours following the assault. Surveillance logs from within Cloud Tower suffered unexplained corruption. Most unsettling, no confirmed visual of Samson’s body has been publicly released, only the statement of his death. With Heaven’s Fall declared eradicated, the war presses forward, one less faction in the shadows. Still, some wonder whether Samson truly fell that day… or if something else ended inside Cloud Tower.
