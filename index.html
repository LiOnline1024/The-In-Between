By Marcus Leon Camus |

The In-Between is a Sci-Fi Action Novel, focusing on Eric Samson.On the war-torn continent of Lyda, 15-year-old Eric joins a military academy to help save his failing nation, forming close bonds with his squad and dreaming of a future beyond the battlefield. But everything shatters when he awakens years older, transformed into a cyborg and hunted by the very people he once trusted. As fragments of a forgotten past resurface, Eric is pulled into a mystery surrounding a figure named Leo and a catastrophic event known only as “The Fall.”
