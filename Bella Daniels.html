The Ghost of the Fall

"I was made to be a weapon, but I only kill for milord"

Bella Daniels

Isolation was all Bella knew. As a child, she was experimented on by a research group, and was turned into a weapon. But when she was rescued by Leo, the leader of the Heaven's Fall, she was repurposed to be his weapon. She was trained to use a sniper rifle, and practically couldn't miss a shot. She devoted herself to Leo, and saw him as her lord, until the day he vanished. Now, she finds herself continuing her lord's mission, and will carry out his will, by any means necessary.
