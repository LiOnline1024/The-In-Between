The Copper Knight

Many files have been classified in the Vanguard, so not much is known about any of the operatives' outside lives. The Copper Knight's info is the one most easily accessible, being that he's become the representative of the Vaguard. The Knight is the head of Frontline Operations as well as it's Engineering division. It seems he has vast wealth if he is able to build as much factories in Alforna's turf, providing weapons and vehichles for the Alfornan army. He seems quick to be aggressive, with his signature weapon being a peculiar 2 handed sword. And multiple times, he's shown to smoke a lot. His feats in engineering lead to comparsions to Nailtheim's own Fixer, Milan Graham, but the Knight focuses directly on weapons.
