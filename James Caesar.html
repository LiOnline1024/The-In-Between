The King

"Guess Kings are wild, because this King can fit in any suit you want~"

James Caesar

In the land between Alforna and Nailtheim, Battel is forced to be a battlefield for the Two nations. As a result, gangs have taken turf it. And while it's an eat or be eaten world there, only one mann has been ableto tame it. James Caesar, the so called "King" of Battel, makes a name for himself as a playboy, sleeping around with any men and women he meets. But he's not only respected for his flirting. As with the way he fights, the term "ruling with an iron fist" is taken literally. And now the resurgence of an old foe, he and Eric start a Rebellion in order to keep peace within Lyda.
