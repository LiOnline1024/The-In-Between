The Blade

"I actually beleived in you... many times. But not anymore."

The Viridian Blade

Many files have been classified in the Vanguard, so not much is known about any of the operatives' outside lives. The Blade is the head operative of Covert Operations. She is skilled in dual wielding blades, but she never used them single handedly. She's known for her speed and agility, but also for her ruthlessness. She never leaves a footprint and makes sure to leave no witnesses, and is also said to have killed the Fallen Angel himself, Leo.
