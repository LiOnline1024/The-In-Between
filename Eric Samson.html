The Angel

"Born From The Hells, Aim For The Heavens"

Eric Samson

In the halls of the Alfornan Republic Military Academy, you'd be forgiven for thinking Eric is a naive student who's in the wrong school. But in the field, he's a different story, with his quick reflexes and accuracy. But despite that, he's someone that just wishes to hang out with his friends and have fun. But he's forced out his comfort zone when he's put into the field early. As he finds himself grown up, in a robotic body in a world where he's framed as a war criminal, so he seeks to uncover the truth behind his past, and find a way to protect those he cares about. And with the help of his friends, he starts a rebellion to fight for the future of Lyda.
