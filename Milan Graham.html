Milan Graham

In the industrial nation of Nailtheim, Gratech stands at the forefront of technological advancement, largely thanks to Milan Graham, a prodigy who designed a revolutionary exoskeleton used for androids at just 10 years old. Rapidly advancing through her education, she became head of R&D by 15, even after an accident forced her into a cybernetic body. Dedicated to protecting soldiers rather than increasing lethality, she developed innovations like the Graywall firewall and a mining railgun. But at 17, her path shifts when the Director of Nailtheim tasks her with infiltrating the Rebellion under the guise of a traitor to her nation. A task that's harder than she thought.

Likes

  • Inventing anything
  • Hacking
  • Her robotic pet phoenix she named Wright
  • Androids and their bodies
  • Failures that helps her perfect her inventions
  • Weapon designs (just the look, not their function)

Dislikes

  • Killing
  • Fire
  • Having to live without her technology
  • Being treated like a child
  • Someone misuses her inventions
  • The idea of a lost cause
